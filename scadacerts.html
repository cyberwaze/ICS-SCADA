There are three key certifications in ICS/SCADA geared toward control system engineers and other security professionals charged with cybersecurity for control system infrastructures and technology along with automation security:
* Global Industrial Cyber Security Professional (GICSP)
* Global Response and Industrial Defense (GRID)
* Global Critical Infrastructure Protection (GCIP)
